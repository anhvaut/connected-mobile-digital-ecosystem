About

Opt4J is an optimization framework developed at Hardware/Software Co-Design, Department of Computer Science at the University of Erlangen-Nuremberg, Germany. The framework is tailored for meta-heuristic multi-objective optimization algorithms and their applications such as crafted testcases and real-world problems. Up to now, several publications on international conferences have been published that rely on the Opt4J framework.

It is considered as a matter of courtesy to link and mention Opt4J if it is used in other projects.
